Course Refund Policy

Students who have paid for courses are able to request a full refund (minus a $25 administrative fee) up until exactly one month prior to the beginning of the course. The closer to the start date of a class, the less of a refund we can offer.

The reason for the decreasing refunds is that we begin to purchase supplies, reserve space, and turn away excess students from classes as we approach the start date. If you need to drop out of the class, let us know as soon as possible.

    Refund Eligibility Schedule:

  • Over One Month = Full refund -$25
  • Over One Week = Partial Refund (Amount paid minus $150)
  • Less than one week = Partial Refund (Amount paid minus $300)

Please e-mail payments@saxifrageschool.org if you have questions or need to request a refund.
